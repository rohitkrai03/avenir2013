Problem Statment

Build a manually controled robot which can run across a multiple terrains including sand,water,net,concrete and rails. It will have to climb up/ down rough/non uniform inclinations.

Rules:


General Rules

  • > The maximum team size is 4.
  • > The participants must be students of same/different college(s).
  • > The participating team found guilty of damaging the arena in anyway will be penalised or even disqualified.
  • > The decisions of the judges will be final and irrevocable.

Game Rules

  • > The event will be a one-on-one event.
  • > The robots should be manually controlled. Not more than two participants can control the robot at any instant of time.
  • > The teams may push, shove, hit or use any mechanism against the other team. But under no circumstances can a team employ any form of cutting mechanism. Use of any such mechanism will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • > The robots will have to start from a designated spot that will be marked on the arena.
  • > There will be checkpoints near both start position but robot can collect only checkpoints of opposite side.
  • > The rules can be modified on spot.

Robot Specification

Robot Specification

  • > The dimensions of the robot must not exceed 35cm(length)X35cm(breadth)X35cm(height) before the start of the event.
  • > The weight of robot must not exceed 10kg.
  • > The participants will be provided with a 220V single phase power supply.
  • > Participants may use wirelessly controlled robots but AVENIR 2012 will not be responsible for any interference caused.

Arena:

Arena Specification

  • > Area covered by arena is 10feetx8feet.
  • > Width of all sections is 40cm.
  • > Maximum depth of water is 10cm.(The purpose of water is only to wet the tyres of robot.)
  • > Arena is having three zones/levels(white,black,red).
  • > Starting position is green coloured area.
  • > Arena can be modified at any time
