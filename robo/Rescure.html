Problem Statment

By virtue of being a top-ranked technical institute in kolkata, NETAJI SUBHASH ENGINEERING COLLEGE and AVENIR feel their obligation towards society to spread the world of technology throughout the globe. Robotics, needless to say, has been the most interesting field of technical advancements in recent times!

Introduction :Once upon a time there was a king.the king had everything he wanted.but one thing the king lacked,he did not have a dream girl so one a day he saddled his strongest horse & departed in search of a perfect queen all alone.After a long time one night the king arrived at a lake and decided to halt& there he saw a beautiful lady.he asked the lady to be queen but she said that she was cursed by a demon.the demon killed her parents and cursed her that she would die if she goes out of lake.but lady told him there was a one way to become free.the king had to travel a rough terrain and collect four blue flower.then he had to climb to the top of the mountain and placed the flower at proper position then he had to dance with some beat of drums until the flower turns red..think u r the king..and play this fabulous robo game.

Objective:To make a manually controlled bot which perform various performance
1.move through various terrain
2.pick up the block and place it another place.
3.At last bot have to make a shape using blocks.

gameplay:

Firstly you have to start from position A. After go through the curvature plane you have to pick up the block go through the terrain .and placed it in position B*.then you have to go through the twisted bridge.and then again you have to pick up thne block and go through the terrain.and then placed it in position E*.and again you have to pick up the block and go through the terrain and placed it F*.Then you have to go on stage and make a triangle and square with some block.

    Robot Specification

    • >The robots should be controlled manually.
    • > The robot must be wired.
    • > The robot cannot have any mechanism that can damage the arena in any manner.
    • > The robots shall be inspected before the event. Failing the inspection shall lead to disqualification.
    • > The machine must not be made from Lego parts, or any ready-made assembly kits. Readily available chassis layouts are not allowed. Any machine having a ready-made chassis will be disqualified.

      • Dimensions and Weight:
    • > The robot should not be more than 400mm*400mm*400mm (length*breadth*height) in size at the start of the event. It can then extend to any height, but the length and breadth should remain in the limit.
    • > The weight of the any robot should not, in any case, exceed 4 kg.

      • Batteries and Power:
    • > Each team must have their own batteries to power their bots.
    • > Voltage must not exceed 24 volts between any two points. External power supply(230v a.c) will be provided.
