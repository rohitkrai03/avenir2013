Problem Statment

BUILD AN AUTONOMOUS ROBOT(LINE FOLLOWER) USING MICROCONTROLLER,TO FOLLOW WHITE LINE ON BLACK SURFACE.

Rules:


General Rule

  • > The maximum team size is 4.
  • > The robot must be autonomous.
  • > The participants must be students of same/different college(s).
  • > The decisions of the judges will be final and binding.

EVENT SPECIFICATIONS

  • > Bot has to be a LINE-FOLLOWER(using MICROCONTROLLER).
  • > Bot has to start from the START position and has to stop at the STOP position.
  • > line will be of 2cm width.
  • > A gap of 10cm is there in the path, the bot has to traverse it to the STOP point.
  • > check points are horizontal strip of white lines of 4cm, the bot has to detect the strip.
  • > Detection can be shown by glowing led or buzzer.
  • > The room in which the event will take place will be relatively dark.

  • > Power can be onboard as well as external power supply can be used.
