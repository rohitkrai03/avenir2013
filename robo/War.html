Robowar (hell in the cell)

Problem Statment

Build a manual robot that can combat with the opponent in the provided area under certain rules.

Rules:

General Rules

  • > The maximum team size is 4.
  • > The participants must be students of same/different college(s).
  • > An individual cannot be part of more than one team.
  • > The participating team found guilty of damaging the arena in anyway will be penalised or even disqualified.
  • > The decisions of the judges will be final and irrevocable.
  • > Organizers reserve the right to change any of the above rules they deems to be fit.
  • > Arena can be change at any point of time of the competition.
  • > Participants must bring the valid identity card of their institute.

Robot Specification


Specification:

  • > The dimension of the robot must be 35cm x 35cm x 35cm at any point of the event.
  • > Maximum weight allowed is 20 kg including on board batteries. Weight of remote controller will not be counted.

Mobility:

  • > Robot should have visible and controlled mobility in order to compete.
  • > Motion of robot should be continous. Jummping, hopping, or flying is not allowed.

Robot Control requirements:

  • > Robot can be wire less or wired.
  • > In wired remote control the potential difference of the input in the remote should not exceed 24V at any point of the event.
  • > In wireless remote the input may exceed 12V.
  • > Damage to arena will cause to instant disqualification.

Battery and Power:

  • > We will provide only one plugin point of 220 v ac supply for each team.
  • > On board batteries must be sealed.
  • > All efforts must be made to protect battery fire, failing to do so will lead into direct sisqualification
  • > Use of damaged and leaked batteries is not allowed.
  • > Change of batteries will not be allowed during the match.
  • > Batteries of bot should be on board only.

Hydraulics:

  • > Liqid used in hydraulics should be inflammable.
  • > Pressure maximum allowed is 1bar.

Weapon System:

  • > Robot can have any type of weapon system.
    ex: magnetic weapons, cutters, flippers, saws, lifting devices, hammers etc but with the following.

Limitations:

  • > Wire cutting not allowed.
  • > No weapon should come in contact with arena at any point of time.
